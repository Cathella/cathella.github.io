UList

UList is an e-commerce platform that aims to simplify the back-to-school shopping experience for students and parents alike. With UList, users can create customized shopping lists for all their school-related needs using a web or mobile application. The platform offers a user-friendly interface that enables customers to easily browse through a wide range of products and add items to their lists.

Once the user has compiled their shopping list, UList takes care of the rest, sourcing the items on the list and delivering them to the user's doorstep, eliminating the need for tedious trips to the store or shops.
